Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate Chip Cookies are the most popular cookie for a reason! Buttery and soft with the perfect amount of chocolate chips, in 20 minutes!

Ingerdients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter , softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar , packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Whisk flour, garlic salt, and pepper in a medium bowl.
  2. Add the chicken to a bowl along with the egg and coat well.
  3. Remove each piece of chicken and coat with the flour mixture then place chicken on a baking sheet until all the pieces are coated.
  4. Let chicken sit for 5 minutes while you heat up your oil (this helps coating stick better).
  5. Heat 2 inches of oil to 350 degrees in a large dutch oven.
  6. Cook the chicken in small batches (do not crowd the pot) for 3-4 minutes until the chicken is cooked to 165 degrees and golden brown and crispy.
